Find Your Perfect Hairstyle Based on Your Head Shape

Last updated: May 20, 2024 | 8 minutes read

Choosing the perfect hairstyle isn't just about following trends; it's about finding what complements your unique features. Understanding your head shape can revolutionize your hairstyling journey, guiding you toward the most flattering cuts and styles. Here’s how you can determine your head shape and choose the best hairstyle accordingly.

Determining Your Head Shape

Measure Your Face:

  • Forehead: Measure across the widest part.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across the prominent part of your cheeks.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to below your ear at the jaw’s base.
  • Face Length: Measure from the top of your forehead to the bottom of your chin.

Identify the Shape:

  • Oval: Face length is greater than the width of the cheekbones, and the forehead is wider than the jawline.
  • Round: Cheekbones and face length have similar measurements, and they are larger than the forehead and jawline.
  • Square: All measurements are fairly similar, with a sharp jawline.
  • Rectangle (Oblong): Face length is the largest measurement, with similar forehead, cheekbone, and jawline widths.
  • Diamond: Face length is the largest, followed by cheekbones, forehead, and the smallest is the jawline.
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the cheekbones and jawline, with a pointed chin.

Hairstyles for Different Head Shapes

Oval Shape:

Best Styles: Almost any hairstyle works. Try layers, waves, or a classic bob.

Avoid: Styles that cover your perfect symmetry.

Round Shape:

Best Styles: Long layers, side bangs, and angled bobs. Volume on top can elongate your face.

Avoid: Round cuts and chin-length bobs that can make your face look fuller.

Square Shape:

Best Styles: Soft layers, side-swept bangs, and textured styles. These soften your strong jawline.

Avoid: Straight bangs and blunt cuts that accentuate your jaw.

Rectangle Shape:

Best Styles: Soft waves, curls, and layered cuts that add width and volume to the sides.

Avoid: Lengthy straight styles that elongate your face further.

Diamond Shape:

Best Styles: Hairstyles that add width at the forehead and chin areas, such as bangs or chin-length bobs.

Avoid: Styles that heighten cheekbones or add volume at the sides, which can accentuate the narrow forehead and chin.

Heart Shape:

Best Styles: Chin-length or longer cuts, side-parted styles, and long bangs. These balance the width of the forehead with the narrowness of the chin.

Avoid: Short, blunt bangs and styles that add volume to the upper face.
