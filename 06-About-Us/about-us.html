ABOUT US

Welcome to Fryzerium 2.0, where we're dedicated to delivering exceptional hair care experiences tailored just for you. Our team is committed to providing you with top-notch service and expert guidance to help you achieve your hair goals.

What stands behind Fryzerium 2.0?

MISSION

What do we do?

We empower individuals to embrace their unique beauty through personalized hair care experiences, exceptional service, and expert guidance on hair styling and maintenance.

VISION

How do we do it?

Our team provides individualized treatments, and expert advice to ensure that each client receives tailored solutions that enhance their natural beauty and complement their personal style.

PURPOSE

Why do we do it?

We believe that everyone deserves to feel confident and beautiful. We aim to uplift and inspire our clients, helping them discover their true beauty and express themselves authentically.

OUR STORY

Founded by Joanna and Lukasz, Fryzerium 2.0 represents the culmination of years of passion, dedication, and expertise in the hairdressing industry. With over 20 years of combined experience, Joanna and Lukasz have built a reputation for excellence, creativity, and innovation in hair styling.

MEET JOANNA

With 14 years of experience running a hairdressing salon, Joanna's passion for hair coloring and styling knows no bounds.

Her dedication to her craft is fueled by the satisfaction of seeing her clients leave the salon feeling confident, beautiful, and refreshed. For Joanna, hairdressing isn't just a job—it's a lifelong passion.

Joanna's meticulous attention to detail has earned her a loyal following of clients who trust her expertise.

MEET ŁUKASZ

Inspired by the world of high fashion, Lukasz brings almost 7 years of experience and a keen eye for detail to Fryzerium 2.0.

His love for hair coloring and his ability to connect with clients on a personal level make every visit to the salon an unforgettable experience.

Lukasz's creativity knows no bounds, and his dedication to his clients' satisfaction is unwavering.
