PRODUCTS

SHAMPOO

Suitable for all hair types, it is perfect for daily use. Cleanses your hair gently, keeping the amount of water naturally contained in the hair unchanged, helping you to prevent the causes of annoying frizz at the root, ensuring balanced hydration.

Furthermore, thanks to its formula enriched with organic Argan Oil and Linseed Oil, you will find new softness and shine with each wash.

CONDITIONER

Choose Nashi Argan Conditioner in case of an undisciplined, dull hair, damaged by coloring or if you are seeking for more nourishment!

In addition to discipline and detangling effect, it guarantees reparation, luminosity and color protection, with a long lasting effect, even after rinsing. For a matchless softness!

INSTANT

Nashi Argan Instant, detangle your hair without rising it off, is the most versatile treatment in the line. It will conquer a special place in your beauty routine, thanks to its effectiveness and speed of use.

In just a few sprays it helps eliminate annoying frizz, prevents split ends, and protects your hair from heat sources. A perfect ally, to always have on hand, and revive your look wherever you are!

HAIR OIL

No-rinse Hair Oil contains the secret to nourishment, volume and a touch of shine in just a few drops, leaving your hair incredibly light!

Thanks to its special formula enriched with certified Organic Argan Oil and Linseed Oil, Nashi Argan Oil reduces drying time, allowing you to achieve a perfect blow-dry in minutes!

HAIR MASK - DEEP INFUSION

Nashi Argan Deep Infusion is the professional mask that you will fall in love with, thanks to its ease of use and immediate results.

It is designed to be used once a week instead of the Conditioner or as an intensive treatment, especially under stressful periods. Deep Infusion penetrates deeply into the hair, nourishing and restoring the ideal hair’s structure without weighing it down.

LOVE HAIR MIST

Nashi Argan LOVE Hair Mist is the hair fragrance to relive the warmth of the Nashi Argan notes with every spray, wherever you are!

Sensual and discreet, it is perfect to carry in your handbag and to wear on your hair whenever you want: in a single gesture it wraps your hair in the fascinating Nashi Argan fragrance, giving it silkiness and an incredible softness, for a long-lasting scent that won’t make you pass unnoticed!
